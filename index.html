PREPARATION INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Lubricate the form with oil and sprinkle with flour.

2. Mix cocoa with water until a paste is formed. Set aside. In a medium bowl, grind sugar and butter together. Add the eggs, one at a time, then add the vanilla. Mix flour with baking powder, add to the oil mixture and mix well. Gradually pour the milk, stirring constantly, until the mixture becomes homogeneous.

3. Put half the dough into a second bowl. Add cocoa and mix well. Fill the mold with 3/4.

4. Bake the cake for 20-25 minutes. After the capkaki completely cool, cover them with your favorite cream.

ADVICE TO RECIPE

The cream recipe (I want to warn you that it turns out to be fat enough). Ingredients: 150 g of sugar; 6 tablespoons of water; 1 packet of vanilla extract; 3 yolks; 220 g of butter (soften to room temperature). Cooking method. In a small saucepan, mix the sugar with water, put on fire and cook for a few minutes to make a syrup. Check the syrup for readiness: the syrup should be stretched with a thin thread. Beat the yolks in a lush, light mass, gradually add the syrup, whisk until the mass becomes lush. Beat the mixer with oil in a lush mass, add yolk mass, beat well. It turns out a gentle cream cream.
